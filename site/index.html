Our Mission

Kollel Nacahalas Tzvi was established in ירושלים עיה"ק in the year תשס"ג 2005 at the behest of Rabbi Yitzchok Alster, שליט"א - a close and beloved talmid of Rav Yitzchok Hutner, זצ"ל. The Kollel recieved the warm blessings of Rav Moshe Shterbuch, שליט"א and has been thriving since its inception.

Avreichim are encouraged to learn at their own pace and master the material learned while highlighting their personal chiddushim in the form of a chaburah or written shtick'l torah.

A special emphasis is placed on utilizing the growth in learning to foster a closeness with the Borei Olam, as stated in the Birchas HaTorah "כלנו יודעי שמך". The Torah learning is coupled with מידות טובות and the ehrlechkeit expected of true Bnei Torah.



Meet The Guys

The Avreichem of Kollel Nachalas Tzvi heil from across the globe. Americans, English, South Africans, Belgians, and Israelis join together to unearth the roots that lie beneath each sugyah; one sugyah at a time.

These dedicated young men are מוסר נפש daily to harver in Torah day and night in favor of seeking more worldly pursuits. Their hard work pays off as we have seen many of the alumni go on to fields of Chinuch, Avodas Hakllal, or leading members in their perspective communities, shining as a beacon of light onto spiritual wastelands.
